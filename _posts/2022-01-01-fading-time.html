Fading Time - A wristwatch with an e-paper watch face that fades between black and white values.

Product description

Fading time is an e-paper wristwatch concept. It suits as a modern alternative to mechanical watches since it has a customizable non-backlit home screen with digital numbers but retains the minimalism of conventional timepieces. The display has to be updated only periodically which allows for a long battery life. The symmetrical frame sits naturally on both wrists.

The name comes from the screen technology since the pixels fade from one color to another. The watch supports wireless charging through the front glass.
